Systems Engineering Development =

20 years ago, I wrote my absolute first piece of code in visual basic. I was a child then, given access to his dad's old computer who managed to install a now ancient copy of visual studio. I started by following tutorials to build things that seemed interesting and grew up into an adult with a passion for all things technology.

10 years ago, I took programming classes and self-taught myself Python, realizing just how many amazing things I could accomplish with the skill.4 years afterward I was combining my development skills with my growing systems engineering skills to improve everything I helped develop and manage, and haven't stopped since.
